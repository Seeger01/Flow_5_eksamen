Learning process

What I've learned during the course?

During my first semester, I have learned to use a lot of new programs, including adobe illustrator,Dreamweaver, experience design, Photoshop and GitHub. In addition, we have learned to write HTML5 CSS3 and JavaScript. However, I still feel that I am a beginner, but I also feel I've learned a lot since I started. We've also had a lot about wireframes, mood boards, mockups, colors, typography, information architecture, PBS and WBS and much more.

How is it related to my prior knowledge of the subject and this task?

For this task, I'll use most of what I've learned when I'm going to make re-designs for all the 4 previous flow projects and I'm going to make almost a whole new website for the exam. I did not have much advance knowledge when I started. The only thing had worked with before was Photoshop. All the other stuff was otherwise mostly new to me.

Visual impression

I have chosen to go with Lato as my main font and ariel as my second, because i think it is easy to read and steady on the eyes. The textcolor is white (#ffffff) so i had to find a good dark background color. I chose dark blue (#001931). There are also some grey (#9a9a9a) in the design both in the gallery and as a hover and border effect. I have chosen this to spice the blue color up a bit.
